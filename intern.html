Molecules on Molecules on Molecules

The Reply

Only two professors responded back. I had sent a mass email to about 30 or so Stanford Mechanical Engineering professors asking if I could do research for them over the summer, and only two professors replied back to me. One of them was retired, and told me he couldn't help me because he was retired. The second professor was Professor Cai and he asked me to meet with him to see if I had an interest in his work.


A Nervous Start

I met with Professor Cai and he told me he does research on molecular dynamics. I didn't remember anything about molecules from high school chemistry and thought that I wouldn't be able to handle this potential internship if he offered it to me. I told him that I was interested, though, and would work hard to make sure I understood the concepts and code for this work. He seemed pleased with my determination and offered me the research internship. I knew that I couldn't disappoint him now.
