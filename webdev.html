Hello World

Callback Me Maybe

After taking a class on Javascript during my Spring Quarter of Freshman year, I realized that I finally had all the tools needed to build my own personal website. However, I was intimidated by other CS majors and thought that I could never work on my own project because I'm not as good as a programmer as they are.


A New Career

Luckily, one of my friends brought me to a hackathon and forced me to build him a website. I did so and that's when I realized how easy it was now that I had learned Javascript. Right after the hackathon, I went straight to work on my own website and finished it within a week. After publishing it on the web, one of my friends thought I did a really good job and wondered if I could build a website for the mom's cafe. I immediately jumped on this opportunity and built the website for her within a couple of weeks. She was so happy about it and I loved seeing how excited my client was about a project I completed.

After creating my first two websites, I then decided to employ myself as a freelance web designer. Now I have two clients that I work for and have been designing their website for over 3 months. I'm glad that my friend pushed me to pursue web development and not give up on my goals because of an insecurity.
